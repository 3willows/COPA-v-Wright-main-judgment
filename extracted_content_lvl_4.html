The Trial bundles were electronic on the Opus2 platform. I have included many bundle references so that the parties can identify the document and page to which reference is made. References to the Trial bundle take the form: {Bundle / Tab / Page}. References to witness statements and expert reports give the name and number of the statement / report e.g. {Wright1 [48]}. References to the transcript take the form {DayX/page:line}.
